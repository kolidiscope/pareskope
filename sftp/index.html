Secure Data Upload via SFTP

Upload your financial datasets securely to Pareskope using SFTP. This method provides encrypted transfer, direct integration with your automation systems, and comprehensive audit logging.

Upload Process in 4 Steps

1

Request Credentials

Contact our support team to obtain your unique SFTP credentials including hostname, username, and SSH key.

2

Configure Connection

Set up your SFTP client with the provided credentials. We support standard SFTP clients and automation scripts.

3

Upload Your Data

Transfer your datasets using standard SFTP commands or your preferred tool. All transfers are encrypted and logged.

4

Verify & Deploy

Our team validates your data and deploys it to the platform. You'll receive confirmation and access details.

Connection Details

Use these details to connect your SFTP client or automation tool:

Server: sftp.pareskope.com
Port: 22
Protocol: SFTP (SSH File Transfer Protocol)
Authentication: SSH Key (asymmetric) or Password
Encryption: TLS 1.3

Contact support@pareskope.com for your unique credentials and SSH public key setup instructions.

Security Features

Encrypted Transfer

All data in transit uses TLS 1.3 encryption. SFTP protocol ensures your datasets are protected from interception.

Authentication

Strong SSH key-based authentication with optional password protection. Multi-factor authentication available for administrative access.

Isolated Directories

Each customer has a dedicated, isolated directory. No cross-tenant data access. Automatic validation prevents malformed uploads.

Audit Logging

Complete audit trail of all upload activities including timestamps, source IPs, file sizes, and checksums for compliance.

Need SFTP Credentials?

To request SFTP access and obtain your connection credentials, please reach out to our support team. We'll configure your account and provide secure setup instructions.
